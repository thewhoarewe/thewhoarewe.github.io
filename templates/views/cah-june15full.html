Great night @ The Coach & Horses!

Last Saturday night we played a loud and rocking set at the Coach & Horses in Strood. It was our first proper gig and it couldn't have been better!

Great support from Manic & The Madkats, The Furry Love Lickers and G.O.D. All these bands were awesome - do yourself a favour and go see them!

Big thanks to Paul Crowhurst for organising a great night, Kev Hammond for looking after the desk, and to the guys from G.O.D for letting us use their rig.
